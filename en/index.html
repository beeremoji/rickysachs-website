About

Country Music from Germany

Ricky Sachs is a country singer and singer-songwriter from Aalen, Baden-Württemberg. He writes his own songs in English, plays acoustic guitar and performs solo - one man, one mic, one guitar.

As one of the few authentic country musicians from Germany, Ricky Sachs stands in the tradition of American heartland country. His sound recalls Morgan Wallen, Tyler Childers and Luke Combs - raw, direct, no radio polish.

The songs are about real moments: homesickness, goodbyes, what stays behind. Country music in English, written in southern Germany. No costume, no gimmick - just the song.

Ricky Sachs plays festivals, town fairs and private events across Germany. As an acoustic solo act, the setup is flexible - the impression stays.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a country singer from Germany?

Yes - country music doesn't only come from Nashville. Ricky Sachs from Aalen writes his own songs in English, plays acoustic guitar and performs solo. The sound is influenced by artists like Morgan Wallen, Tyler Childers and Luke Combs.

What kind of music does Ricky Sachs make?

Acoustic country and Americana in English. Original songs, selected covers - solo with guitar and voice. No band setup, no playback tracks. Influenced by American heartland country.

Can you book Ricky Sachs for festivals and events?

Yes. Ricky plays festivals, town fairs, wine festivals, corporate events and private parties across Germany. As a solo act, he only needs a PA system on site. Send requests via the contact form or email booking@rickysachs.com.

Why does Ricky Sachs sing in English?

Because that's how the songs come to him. Country music in English sounds the way country is meant to sound - the language shapes the rhythm and the feel. Ricky writes in the language he hears the music in. Not a concept, not a decision - just the way it is.

Where can you see Ricky Sachs live?

Current dates are listed in the Shows section. In 2026, including the US Car Rollin Festival in Fachsenfeld. Current updates on Instagram: @ricky.sachs.
